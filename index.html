Hi-I'm Gabe

I am currently in my undergrad studying Mechatronics
Engineering at the University of Waterloo



I have a passion for robotics, AI, and everything that comes with it. I am inspired by humanoid robotics, biomimetic robots, and mechanical/software innovations in general. My experience spans machine vision, aerial robotics, and hardware/controls integration, and I am eager to gain more skills and accomplish more in these areas.



Projects I have worked on for fun include fixed-wing planes, quadcopters, a hexcopter, robot arms, LED cubes, and other robots. At previous jobs, I have been involved in integrating machine vision solutions in large medical device production lines, integrating cameras and vision systems, and configuring software for various inspections. I have also been involved in assembly of hydraulic pipe systems at wastewater treatment plants, and using surveying equipment to layout various features for the renovation of a municipal pumping station.
